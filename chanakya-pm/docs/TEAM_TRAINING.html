Section 01

Sign in and navigate

Sign in with Google (preferred)

  1. Open chanakya-pm.onrender.com
  2. Click Sign in with Google, choose your @whitelotusgroup.in account

First time sign in auto creates your account with edit access. Need more, ask your admin.

Email and password

Enter your work email and the shared default password from your admin. Forgot yours, contact your admin. Self reset is not yet built.

Session

  • Signed in for seven days
  • Warning banner fifteen minutes before expiry so you can save and sign in again

Navigating

You land on Select Development. Choose Amanvana, Kandhavara, or View All. Click a villa card to open its dashboard. The badge next to the Chanakya logo shows the current development, click it to go back.

Section 02

Daily tasks

Add a task edit

  1. Villa, WBS tab, Add Task (top right)
  2. Fill name, phase, dates, status, progress. Optional. predecessors, assignee, cost.
  3. Save. Auto syncs within 1.5 seconds.

Edit a task

Click the task row, the panel opens on the right. Edit fields, changes save automatically. On the Gantt, drag a bar left or right to shift dates. Duration is preserved, and an undo button appears.

Status, blocked, delete

  • Change Status from the dropdown
  • For blocked or on hold, a reason field appears. Fill it in.
  • Delete shows a toast with Undo for six seconds. Admins can restore later via the audit log.

Baselines edit

Open the Baselines tab, click Capture New Baseline, name it, save. Baselines are read only snapshots. Compare any two to see schedule changes.

Import, export, audit manage

  • Import. CSV or Excel. Three modes. Column mapper with preview.
  • Export. Downloads a full JSON backup. Every export is logged.
  • Audit log. Last five hundred actions. Who changed what, when.

approveSee the Approvals panel. Approve or reject requests.
manageCreate villas, Import, Export, Add Development, Audit Log, Manage panel.

Permissions are independent. A user can have any combination.

Working together

  • Every change auto saves every 1.5 seconds. Others see it on reload or next save.
  • If two people edit the same villa at once, whoever saves second is asked to Reload, then redo the edit. No data is silently lost.
  • If the connection drops, a banner retries every five seconds. After thirty seconds it turns red and asks you to stop editing until the network returns.
  • Every create, edit, delete, reschedule, import, export, and approval is logged with your name and timestamp.

Known limits

  • Desktop and laptop only. Mobile is not yet supported.
  • No self service password reset. Contact your admin.
  • No photo or document attachments. Use the shared drive.
  • Live updates appear on reload or next save, not instantly.
  • Bulk task edit is limited to Gantt drag for date shifts.

Support

All issues route to your project admin. For bugs, include a screenshot and the DevTools Console output so the fix can go in quickly.
