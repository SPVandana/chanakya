Section 01

Signing in

Google sign in

Preferred for everyone at White Lotus Group.

  1. Open chanakya-pm.onrender.com
  2. Click Sign in with Google
  3. Choose your @whitelotusgroup.in work account

First time sign in auto creates your account with edit access. Need more, ask Vandana.

Email and password

  1. Enter your work email
  2. Enter the shared default password from Vandana
  3. Click Sign In

Forgot your password, email Vandana. Self reset is not yet available.

Session

  • You stay signed in for seven days
  • A warning banner appears fifteen minutes before expiry so you can save and sign in again
  • If a session expires mid edit, you are returned to the login screen with a clear notice

Section 03

Daily tasks

Add a task edit

  1. Villa dashboard, WBS tab
  2. Click Add Task, top right
  3. Fill in name, phase, dates, status, progress
  4. Optional, add predecessors, assignee, cost, notes
  5. Click Save. Auto saves within 1.5 seconds.

Edit a task

  1. Click the task row. The task panel opens on the right.
  2. Edit fields, changes save automatically
  3. Or click the task name to open the full modal

On the Gantt, drag a bar left or right to shift dates. Duration is preserved. An undo button appears.

Status, blocked, delete

  • Change Status from the dropdown
  • For blocked or on hold, a reason field appears. Fill it in so teammates know why.
  • Delete shows a toast with Undo for six seconds. Admins can restore later via the audit log.

Baselines edit

  1. Open the Baselines tab
  2. Click Capture New Baseline
  3. Name it, add notes, save

Baselines are read only snapshots. Compare any two to see schedule changes.

Import, export, audit log manage

  • Import. CSV or Excel, three modes, column mapper with preview.
  • Export. Downloads a full JSON backup. Every export is logged.
  • Audit log. Last five hundred actions, who changed what and when.

Section 05

Working together

Auto save and conflicts

  • Every change auto saves to the server every 1.5 seconds
  • Other team members see your changes on reload or next save
  • If two people edit the same villa at once, whoever saves second sees the banner Another user saved changes after you last synced. Reload to see the latest, then retry your edit.
  • Click Reload, redo your edit. No data is silently lost.

Offline and bad network

  • If the connection drops, a banner reads Your changes are not syncing to the server. Retrying.
  • The app retries automatically every five seconds
  • If saves keep failing for thirty seconds or more, the banner turns red and asks you to stop editing until the connection returns
  • Click Retry now to force a retry, or Reload to start fresh

What is logged

Every create, edit, delete, reschedule, import, export, and approval is logged with your name and a timestamp. Admins can review the log at any time.

  • No self service password reset. Email Vandana.
